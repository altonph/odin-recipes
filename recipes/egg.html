Egg Drop Soup

Egg drop soup is a quick, easy and comforting soup that many are familiar with

it is a classic in Chinese American cuisine as a side dish that many enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch (mixed with 1/3 cup water)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 green onion/scallion

Steps

  1. Bring the chicken stock to a simmer in a medium soup pot.
  2. Stir in the sesame oil, salt, sugar and white pepper
  3. Add cornstarch and water slurry
  4. Mix and simmer until to desired consistency
  5. Mix in lighty beaten egg until egg is fully cooked
  6. Add in chopped green onions and serve!
