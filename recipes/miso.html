Miso Soup

Authentic Japanese miso soup

It is easy to make miso soup at home! In this recipe i will show you how to make yummy and comforting soup stock

from scratch and then making the classic miso soup with tofu and wakame seaweed. Miso soup is not only delicious,

but also brings many great health benefits.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 Tbsp instant dashi powder
  • 7 oz soft/silken tofu (kinugoshi dofu)
  • 4-5 Tbsp miso
  • 1 Tbsp dried wakame seaweed
  • 1 green onion/scallion

Steps

  1. Add 4 cups water and 1 tablespoon of instant dashi powder to a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil slowly on medium-low heat then simmer on medium
  3. Add 4-5 tbsp of miso through a fine mesh strainer
  4. Add in cubed tofu, dried wakame seaweed and green onions
  5. Serve and enjoy!
